Help

Adding a new instance

- to add a new instance, go to instances page

- click on the 'Add New Instance' button in the right section of the page

- enter base url, time zone and credentials for your JIRA server and click 'Save'

- click on the 'Activate subscription' button that will create a paypal subscription for the new instance (please note that the list of JIRA servers will be updated after the subscription is processed)

Setting up the report for project

- go to the Projects page and click on the project you want the report set for

- fill in the 'Send at' field with the exact hour you want to receive the daily report. By clicking 'Save settings' at this point, the daily report is active and will be sent every day with the default settings

- to deactivate the report, just leave the field with the report time empty

- by default, the Draft Report will be sent to the project manager, and the Final Report to all active users in the project. You can easily choose which group of people you want to receive the report, in the 'Recipients' section. By clicking 'Send full draft/final report to others' you can add new email adresses that will also receive the report

- you may set the number of estimated hours that your team will work per day and per month, to easily keep track of your project status, in the 'Planning' section

- you can integrate your JIRA project with GitHub or SVN in the 'Source control' section. Fill in with the repository details and credentials, and click on 'Check availability'

- if you successfully added the source control account, we recommend mapping each JIRA user with a source control account, where possible, in the 'User options' section

- you cand also exclude an user from the report or change the email adress where he will receive the report (by default, each user will receive the daily report at the email adress he used to sign-up on JIRA)

- you may override estimated work rate for each month, and also provide a list with free days for the team so that the report will not be sent

- 'Reopened tasks status' represents the status of the reopened tasks

- you can choose to not receive individual report and neither a full draft in the 'Draft options', at the bottom of the settings page

- by default, Saturday and Sunday are considered weekend days, and the daily report will not be sent. You can choose which days you consider free during the week

- you have the option to choose which issue statuses you want displayed in the email report along with the task details, in the 'Workflow statuses' field. Enter just one status per line
