Email based

Each team member receives the report in their inbox every day. It looks great on most email clients. Mobile too. Our unique 3 step process is 100% email driven. No sign in required for you, your team, your boss or your clients.

Do it yourself

You don't need to be the admin of your JIRA site to make it work. If you have read access to a project than you can start getting the daily report for that project. You can activate no matter if you are a project manager, developer or client.

30 second set up

It's as easy as setting the report time for your project. That's actually the only thing you need to set to make it work. We have clever defaults for everything else. Also, once you've set it up, you can just forget about it. The report will just show up in your inbox every day.
