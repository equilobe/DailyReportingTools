Sprint status

Immediately see if your sprint is on track or if you need to adjust scope. Chart values are all daily averages for easy comparison. A month chart is also present, because most of the time budgets are monthly even though you work in sprints.

Team summary

Review the work breakdown for each team member with an easy to read chart that uses only daily averages. Immediately spot if someone forgot to log work or has too much work left.

“Scrum notes”

See the tasks and sub-tasks that a person worked on the day before, as indicated by his work logs. The list of commits and pull requests are included by connecting your version control system. You can also see what the person will work on next.

Recently completed

Review the tasks that have been completed in the last 7 days. The responsible person and how long it took to complete the task is also shown.
